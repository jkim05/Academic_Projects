Blockchain gained great interest from a variety of industry sectors such as: finance, health care, food processing, power, and energy markets (Gourisetti et al., 2020). The healthcare sector has been exploring how blockchain technology can aid in the sharing of medical records and other confidential information. Due to the pandemic, there has been a renewed interest in utilizing telehealth services to reduce community disease spread (Tan et al., 2020). Discussions of medical devices with internet capability being used to securely store and upload sensitive patient information with the use of a permissions blockchain platform. This allows healthcare professionals to have the ability to remotely access patient data easily and securely. Creating an improved healthcare infrastructure for the patient and clinician.
